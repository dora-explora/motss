Speaking of weird laws, I understand that in San Francisco it is illegal to
sleep in the nude.  The reason given is that during the 1906 fire and
earthquake, people were awakened and ran into the streets.  A lot of them
were nude.  The law was passed so that this wouldn't happen again.

I guess it scared a lot of horses or something.
