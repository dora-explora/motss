Yup, there's only one of me.  Actually, I try to avoid being a member of
the "top 25 users" list every month, since I think that this is a dubious
honor (I don't expect to avoid it this month.)  On the other hand, I do most
of my non-technical reading and writing late in the evening, so it doesn't
interfere too much with my work or my personal life.  Naturally, if writing
comes easy to you, a 40 line (or more) article isn't too time consuming.

This recent prolix period in net.motss and net.singles arose because I
was interested in the issues and I felt I had something to contribute.
This is what I always use as a guide to whether I should say something.
Net.motss had gone through a significant dry spell during the winter and
early spring, and it is wonderful to see new people and new issues appearing.
