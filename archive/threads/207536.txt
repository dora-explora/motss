Recent Historical Studies of Sex & Sexuality
============================================

The following bibliography is from Lawrence Stone's survey/review
"A History of Sex" in a recent issue of The New Republic (p 26).

Much of the research deals with religion & law.

The order is chronological by subject.

(Printed without permission.)

-------------------------------------------------------------------

WESTERN SEXUALITY: PRACTICE & PRECEPT IN PAST & PRESENT TIMES edited
by Philippe Aries & Andre Bejin (Basil Blackwell, 1985, 220 pp.)

GREEK HOMOSEXUALITY by K. J. Dover (Harvard University Press, 1978,
288 pp.; Vintage Books, 1978, 244 pp.)

THE REIGN OF THE PHALLUS: SEXUAL POLITICS IN ANCIENT ATHENS by Eva
C. Keuls (Harper & Row, 1985, 452 pp.)

L'HISTOIRE DE LA SEXUALITE: 2. L'USAGE DES PLAISIRS, 3. LE SOUCI DE
SOI by Michel Foucault (Gallimard, 1984, 285 pp., 284 pp.)

Unpublished lectures from a forthcoming book, VIRGINITY AND SOCIETY:
MEN, WOMEN, AND SEXUAL RENUNCIATION IN LATE ANTIQUITY by Peter Brown.

"Homosexuality in ancient Rome" by Paul Veyne in Aries & Bejin.

"La Famille et l'Amour sous le Haut-Empire Romain" by Paul Veyne,
ANNALES ESC, 33, 1978.

CHRISTIANITY, SOCIAL TOLERANCE, AND HOMOSEXUALITY: GAY PEOPLE IN
WESTERN EUROPE FROM THE BEGINNING OF THE CHRISTIAN ERA TO THE
FOURTEENTH CENTURY by John Boswell (University of Chicago Press,
1980, 442 pp.)

PROSTITUTION IN MEDIEVAL SOCIETY: THE HISTORY OF AN URBAN INSTITU-
TION IN LANGUEDOC by Leah Lydia Otis (University of Chicago Press,
1985, 240 pp.)

"Sex in married life in the early Middle Ages" by Jean-Louis Flandrin
in Aries & Bejin.

"Prostitution, sex & society in French towns in the fifteenth century"
by Jacques Rossiaud in Aries & Bejin.

L'EROTISME AU MOYEN AGE edited by Bruno Roy (Editions de L'Aurore,
1977)

THE BOUNDARIES OF EROS: SEX CRIME AND SEXUALITY IN RENAISSANCE VENICE

"La Prostitution Florentine au XVe Siecle" and "Le Celibat" by Richard
Trexler, ANNALES ESC, 36, 1981; 27, 1972.

LES AMOURS PAYSANNES XVIe-XIXe SIECLES by Jean-Louis Flandrin (Gallimard,

LES SEXE ET L'OCCIDENT: EVOLUTION DES ATTITUDES ET DES COMPORTEMENTS

SEXUALITY IN EIGHTEENTH-CENTURY BRITAIN edited by Paul-Gabriel Bouce

UNFIT FOR MODEST EARS: A STUDY OF PORNOGRAPHIC, OBSCENE AND BAWDY WORKS

HOMOSEXUALITY IN RENAISSANCE ENGLAND by Alan Bray (Gay Men's Press, 1982,

HISTOIRE DE LA SEXUALITE: LA VOLONTE DE SAVOIR by Michel Foucault

AT ODDS: WOMEN AND THE FAMILY IN AMERICA FROM THE REVOLUTION TO THE

THE BOURGEOIS EXPERIENCE: VICTORIA TO FREUD. VOLUME I, EDUCATION OF
