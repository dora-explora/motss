A Romantic View on Bisexuality
Anonymous@ima.UUCP
08/20/85
Amidst all this discussion on bisexuality, I just want to share
the opinion of a woman who was taught in college by my father,
and who was very close to me and my family as I was growing up.
She wrote to me recently, and, to my slight surprise, told me
that she was bisexual.  In her view, she said, something so
natural and good as love extends beyond sexual boundaries, and
that love relationships are too important to exclude fifty per
cent of the population.  I admit, it is somewhat an ideal view,
and it couldn't apply to everyone, but it's certainly a beautiful
way to describe bisexuality.

I have written to this newsgroup before and signed my name, but
this time around, I think I should remain anonymous to protect
the innocent.  Cheers to you all.

				    PH
