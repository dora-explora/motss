advice request for gay video series
bothner@Navajo.ARPA
11/20/85
The Gay and Lesbian Alliance at Stanford is trying to
organize a semi-regular film series showing gay-themed
(including Lesbian-themed) movies. I am asking if anyone
out there has any advice on how to get hold of interesting
movies, and in general running such a series.

The main problem is a combination of limited time and money
(and experience). Films where you can legally charge for
admission have high rental costs, which would require a big
publicity effort, and a high risk of losing a lot of money.
So the best bet would seem to be to rent videos, and not
charge admission. This keeps the whole operation low-key,
both in terms of budget and man-power. The problem is that
few rentals (even in the SF Bay Area) have many gay-themed
videos, since most of them are fairly obscure (to the
general audience).

Any help would be welcome. I'll summarize any private mail
I receive.
