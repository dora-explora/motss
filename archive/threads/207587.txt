                   New Special Interest Gay Newsgroups
                   -----------------------------------

   In keeping with the first day of April, which doesn't come until next
year; an entirely new series of newsgroups are being added to USENET to
replace the once devine but now dreary net.motss newsgroup. Due to a huge
number of requests from subscribers for special interest sub-groups, the
new net.newsgroups will be ready on Oscar Wilde day. The once popular
net.motss group met it's demise after a lengthy 18 month discussion on
the subjects; 

* Should quiche be served to straight dinner guests  (12,500 responses)

* Merits of starting Tea dance at 8am instead of     (8735   responses)
  4pm

* Whether to tell God that we're gay                 (15,675 responses)

* 3 Mile Island as a gay resort, would you go?       (27,900 responses)
 
* Breakup of lover relationships caused by a new     (13,667 responses) 
  virus.

* Discussions on Dynasty, Dallas and Falconcrest     (197,890 responses)

up and Mike Simpson developed arthritus in his fringers from trying to
