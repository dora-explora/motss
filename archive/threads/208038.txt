Welcome one and all to--

		     STREET THEATER!!!

A fantastic theatrical production portraying life on Christopher
St during the Stonewall riots.  Yes, it's Doric Wilson's Street
Theater, produced and directed by Alan Dubroc.  Street Theater
will appear at MIT's Kresge Little Theater, next to the MIT
Student Center and just across the street from the Pillars at 77
Mass. Ave in Cambridge.  Tickets are $7 general admission, $5 for
students and seniors--sold only at the door.  Shows are all at
8pm on the following dates:  July 26,27,28 and August 2,3,4.
Here's your chance to celebrate Stonewall in style.  Brought to
you courtesy of GAMIT (Gays at MIT).
