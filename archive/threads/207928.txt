posted anything to this newsgroup for a while.

(i)	How, I repeat, do you know I'm NOT asking a question, and
	genuinely seeking some information?  I've not gone away,
	I'm still here, still reading.  And still waiting, probably

	with homosexual groups for being homosexual groups.
	(As you pointed out, they need not be, although as Ken replied,
	what is to be made of a group with a name such as MAN/BOY?)

	apparently incongruous juxtaposition is not homosexual/heterosexual.
	The relevant dimension in Ken's mind (I'm guessing, of
	course), Dave Brunson's mind, and yes, my mind (but perhaps
	I'm victorian) is normal/perverse, and to large numbers of
	people, such as myself, Dave and (perhaps) Ken, homosexuality
	is perverse.  I hope you do not consider that statement as
	automatically invalidating what I said above in (i).
