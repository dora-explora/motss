Subj: My first submission to net.motss

	Hello.  I see that the discussion in net.motss has fallen off in
recent weeks.  I would like to help remedy that situation.  For my first
submission, I would like to open or perhaps re-open the discussion about
anonymity on net.motss.

	I have been reading net.motss since it first came out.  I have
waited until now to submit because I wanted to submit anonymously.  I
wanted anonymity because several places I have interview at for jobs
receive netnews, and I decided that I wanted them to meet me without
preconceived notions about someone who regularly submits to "That gay
newsgroup."

	There was recently some discussion on a gay bullettin board I
dial in to about anonymity for gay discussion on computers.  The
concensus there was that a bullettin board computer represented a small
community that people felt more comfortable giving out their real names
to than something like net.motss, where large numbers of possibly
unsympathetic people could see their submissions.

	Do the people already submitting to net.motss think that more
effort should be made to help people submit anonymously.  I found the
modifications to news of dubious utility because the machine I use for
news is not sufficiently under my control for me to make the changes,
and those that might make them don't want to know that I am submitting
to "that gay newsgroup".

	Well, enough for now. I will soon be beginning a contest with
debenedi at yale-comix to find out who can submit more things to
net.motss. 

	Until then, take care,
		"Felix"
