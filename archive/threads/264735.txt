	Call For Votes --  rec.roller-coaster

Status: unmoderated

Rationale:   This newsgroup will be for the discussion of
any amusement park rides, including but not limited to, 
roller coasters. Also covered will be operating seasons for
many of the larger parks, admission prices, etc.

Voting Mechanics:     In accordance with the usenet guidelines, 

   Send your votes to rhb@pa.usl.edu

   Please include either a 'yes' or a 'no' in your Subject Line.

   Remember: for your vote to be acceptable it should have wording 
                 similar to:

             I vote for the group rec.roller-coaster as proposed
                   or 
             I vote against the group rec.roller-coaster as proposed


Voting Deadline:

Your vote must be in my mail queue by July 6th, 1991 at midnight CDT 
(Central Daylight Time).
