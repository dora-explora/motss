What do people think of Immigration and Naturalization Service's
decision to deport Australian citizen Tony Sullivan on the
grounds that he is gay, despite the fact the he has been lovers
with an American in California since April 1972 (12 years!!!)???
Tony Sullivan and Richard Adams were *legally* married in
Boulder, CO in 1974.  The INS informed them that, "You have
failed to establish that a bona-fide marital relationship
can exist between faggots."  They reissued the statement without
the word "faggots" after considerable protest activities.

The Supreme Court refused to hear the case of Richard Longstaff,
a gay man who emigrated from Britain 19 years ago.  Texas
Federal Court ruled that Longstaff, who is a Dallas businessman,
could not become a citizen of the US and could not remain
in the country because, although he is of "good moral
character," he has a "psychopathic personality" as a result
of his homosexuality.
