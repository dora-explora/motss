As far as I am concerned, I am glad to see postings in this group from both
heterosexuals as well as homosexuals.  One of the main purposes of this news
group is to allow meaningful dialogue.  And with such dialogue, maybe a better
understanding may develop.  Within the framework of this group as it was
initially established, [ a) not a forum for whether gay is good/bad, natural/
unnatural, and b) the discussion of explicit sexual practices which could be
offensive to some readers will not be allowed.  NOTE: Sorry I'm paraphrasing
these, but don't have the original article to refer to ],   I say, come one, 
come all, join the discussion.  That's what so many people fought so hard for.
This is a forum for dialogue on issues related to homosexuality, but not 
limited to the posting of articles by only homosexuals.
