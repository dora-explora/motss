A number of times in the past, readers of net.motss have made offers to forward
anonymous messages to the newsgroup from people who do not feel comfortable
"coming out", electronically or otherwise.  Here are a few again, originating
from my home machine, 'spdcc'.

In brief, I put these on the net uneditted, though I reserve the right to
refuse to use my machine to originate articles that I personally feel
uncomfortable with.  Usually, the person posting anonymously chooses
a pseudonym, so that personal mail directed back to the author can
be forwarded on and that replies on the net can be disambiguated.
