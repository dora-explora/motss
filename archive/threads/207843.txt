Republicans and the right-wing press are using issues of homosexuality?  The

used to smear the Democrats.

other.  Now it looks as if there are contests on to out-"homo" each other!

Massachusetts, homosexual representative Gary Studds' opponent promised not
to make Studds' homosexuality an issue; but he went around saying that he was
the only candidate with a wife!)

the Copley News Service that was trying to smear Mondale with what it saw as
the least desirable "special interests."  Homosexuals - represented by a mous-
tached man with a dress, purse, and Gay Power button - are, of course, some
of these undesirables.
