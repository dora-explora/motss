An Early Frost - Next Week !
bal@drutx.UUCP (LesebergB)
11/07/85
        According to next week's TV Guide, the  television  movie
"An Early Frost" will air next Monday evening the 11th on NBC.

        This is the first TV-movie to dramatize the plight  of  a
homosexual  man(a  young  lawyer,Michael,  played by Aidan Quinn)
stricken with AIDS.  And it does so with intelligence  and  guts,
says  TV Guide.  Most of the movie focuses on the reaction of the
victim's parents, who didn't  know  he's  gay,  and  on  his  own
psychological trauma.

        The article also made reference to  a  1972  film,  "That
Certain  Summer",  starring  Hal Holbrook and Martin Sheen.  This
was called  TV's  first  frank  and  compassionate  treatment  of
homosexuality.   I  have  never  heard of this movie before.  Has
anyone out there heard of it or seen it ?  How frank of  a  movie
was it for 1972 ?

        "An Early Frost" follows Dick and Ed in "TV's Bloopers  &
Pratical  Jokes."   I  only  mention this because their scheduled
segments  include  bloopers   from   Showtime's   comedy   series
"Brothers."  Coincidence ??????

        Following the local news, a NBC News Special will examine
the  facts and myths about AIDS and discuss that evening's movie,
"An Early Frost."

        That's it for NBC's Monday evening  lineup,  busy  night,
HUH ?!
