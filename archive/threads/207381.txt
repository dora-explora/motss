Well that was a great night on television.

 9 pm - "Dynasty"
        Another of Steven's lovers gets to leave the show by dying. I thought
        death as divine punishment for homosexuality was a thing of the past.
10 pm - "Hotel"
        A judge decides that two successful gay men chosen by an 11 year old
        girl's deceased mother are unfit to be foster parents. Never mind that
        it is also the girl's preference to stay with them. (Also if it hadn't
        been explicitly stated, it would have been difficult to tell that the
        2 guys were lovers since they never touched or said anything to suggest
        it)
11 pm - News
        Rock Hudson dead at 59.

By the way, has anyone heard anything about "Brothers" being syndicated or put
on videotape? I'm not a subscriber to Showtime.
