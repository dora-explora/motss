names in reference to gay people.  I have known only one gay person
named Bruce, and he never acted in any way different from the many other
gay people I know.  There's also a Rick Springfield song called "They
Call Me Bruce", but that's in reference to people confusing him with
Bruce Springsteen, who DEFINITLY does not act gay in any way.
