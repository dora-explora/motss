> should be accepted as a minority with protected rights"!
> You refuse to do what other minorities have done to be accepted - go through
> an extended period of defense and dialog with those who did not accept them.

Mr. Arndt seems to think that minorities have to "pay dues" in order to gain
rights.  That, since blacks, Jews, etc. have gone "through an extended period
of defense and dialog with those who did not accept them", homosexuals (and
any other "up and coming" minority that might wish to be accepted as human
beings) must do so in order to "be accepted as a minority with protected
rights"...

... when in fact, the real purpose behind fighting for the rights of any
minority is (hopefully) to ensure that NO minority need ever have to FIGHT
for rights that they should have simply because they are human beings!!!!
