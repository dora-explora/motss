All this great reading about what we're looking for in a MOTAS and
some recent close encounters have prompted a new selection critera
for my list.  

4.  Emotional strength, endurance, and agressiveness

The last thing I want is an SO that I have to carry like emotional baggage.
Someone who can stand on their own and handle tough times.  Tough situations
can range from staying alone for a weekend, to running a household, to
running a business, to weathering an economic crisis, and even to keeping a
cool head while doing 360's on an icy highway in traffic.

Endurance is just longterm strength.  

Emotional agressiveness is a combination of spunk, politics, and desire for
a fulfilling relationship.  This person doesn't wait for things to happen.
They actively try to explore and better the relationship.  This is hard.
Am I getting this across?
