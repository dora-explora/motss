Here is the schedule of IGRA (International Gay Rodeo Association) sanctioned
rodeos for the '86-'87 season:

Organization	City		Rodeo				Dates

TGRA		Dallas		Texas Gay Rodeo			Nov. 14-17, 1986
AGRA		Phoenix?	Arizona Gay Rodeo		Jan. 26-18, 1987
GSGRA		L.A.		California Gay Rodeo		Mar. 27-29, 1987
CGRA		Denver?		Rocky Mountain Regional Rodeo	May 29-31, 1987
OGRA		OKC?		Great Plains Regional Rodeo	Aug. 7-9, 1987
IGRA/GSGRA	San Francisco	IGRA Finals Rodeo		Sep. 18-20, 1987

The local chapter I belong to, the Bay Area Chapter of the Golden 
State Gay Rodeo Association, will be producing the *first* ever Gay 
Finals rodeo next September. If you live in the Bay Area and would 
like to help (putting on a rodeo and associated social events is a 
monumental task that we are now beginning a year in advance), feel 
free contact me. No experience necessary. Only a willingness to work 
and have fun is required.
