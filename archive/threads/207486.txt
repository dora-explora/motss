Let me expand on what I am saying... Discussing things in a public place
expands EVERYONES understanding. Non Gay readers can find a safe space to ask
questions that are perplexing them about Gay life. Gay people need to deal
with the issue of mis-information all the time and the scope of mis-informed
attitudes prevelant out there is enormous. I might add that mis-information
is as common amongst gays as non gays. As long as Gay people are seen as 
mysterious entitys whose behavior is outside of the experience of non-gays,
then the non-gay population will continue to fear and persecute us as 
unknown quantities. But we are living Human beings, we must humanise our 
position in our culture We must bring our behavior out into the light of 
day and into the view of everyone. I suspect that this process will be painfull
and more than a little provocative, but I am convinced that it is necesary
if we are ever to progress beyond our current status. I believe that those who
criticise the Sister boom booms and others like ter do us a diservice.
They tell us that we will never achieve main stream recognition by flaunting 
our drag queens.. I say we will never recieve recognition thats worth anything
until we show our true selves, and we better be able to accept our own selves
as worthy of love and respect before we do that. Weneed to accept that we are 
the fringe of society and as such are by nature going to be creating new 
frontiers as fast as main stream culture accepts and adopts our old ones.
I think that its no accident that there are so many Gay people at the 
forefront of the creative arts and sciences, and that our sexual preference
reflects that creative impulse in our personal lives. Without pioneers pushing
the bell curve of human experience into uncharted areas life would still be
a static boring affair. The simple truth seems to be that some pioneers
are predisposed to be pioneering with the area of human sexuality and some are 
not. Common sense when unfettered by moral dogma tells us that different is 
not bad its just different, cultural & social customs consist of arbitrary 
values . Sometimes these values are those of a select few who have gained
a powerfull position of influence, these values then become the accepted 
standard by which others within the system are expected to conform. Democracy
purports to elevate the values and wishes of the majority to the position
of rule, though evidence of this actually happening is scant. In either case
the pioneers are left out of the process, except in the rare occurance when
an authority happens to be a fringe explorer instead of the usual mainstream 
type. Some cultures have made the connection that their pioneers are a vital
force for necesary growth and change, and make a safe place within for them.
Often the other aspects of life that are equally mysterious are all linked
together as evidenced by the many shamanic traditions which incorporate 
homosexuality, cross dressing and other nonstandard practises in their system.
American Indian tribes often reserved a place of great honor in their tribal
stucture for the Berdache for example.
