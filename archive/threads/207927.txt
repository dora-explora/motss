I seem to have created the wrong impression with my "if you hand..."
article.  Some people have gotten thhe impression that I am utterly
opposed to homosexuality.

I am in fact in considerable doubt on the subject.  The NT is by no
means clear on the subject, and I shy away from applying Mosiac law
to non-jews.  I have a number of friends who are homosexuals, and I
think that for almost all purposes, there is no reason to discriminate
against them.

On the other hand, the NT is fairly clear about sexual promiscuity.
In that regard, my remark about chastity still applies.

I intend to abstain from this discussion in the future.

Charley Wingate
