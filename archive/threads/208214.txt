"Loving Someone Gay" by Don Clark, PhD.  This is a neat and warm book
about being gay and being comfortable with that, as well as illuminating
the humanness of gay people to non-gay people.  I highly recommend this
book to everybody seeking to understand gayness.

The book is published by Signet, is paperback, brown, and costs $3.50.

Another book is "Now That You Know" by Betty Fairchild who has a gay
son.  She was very active in Parents and Friends of Gays in Denver
and was president of the chapter until she moved to San Francisco.
Although I haven't read the book, I understand it is well written and
gives some excellent advice to parents and friends of gay men and
lesbians.
