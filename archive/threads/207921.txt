     This is my first article to this newsgroup, even though I've been a
watcher for quite a while now. There have been complaints, by those who
regularly submit articles here, that garbage has been the main ingredient
in the last hundred or so postings. Well, this is my attempt to submit
something other than yet another bitter-response-causing/tirade-inducing
letter. So please, take this in the spirit in which it's given.

     During the Olympics this summer, there were many fine men to watch,
and some of them were pointed out on this very newsgroup --- gymnasts
and swimmers, for example. Of course, this kind of socially acceptable
voyeurism can be practised more often than once every four years. My
favorite are football players, especially those in college. It has to
do with which kind of men attract me the most. What about everyone else?
Surely I'm not alone in this. What are your favorite types of athletes
for body watching? If you're not into jocks, no pun intended, what are
your favorite types of men? As you can tell, this is not at all a serious
subject as far as the destiny of the universe is concerned (which some
subscribers to net.motss seem to believe is the only thing worth discussing),
but rather it's a fun subject, meant to do nothing more than stimulate
your ... uh ... imagination. Let's hear it from the boys!

Gregg Loring
