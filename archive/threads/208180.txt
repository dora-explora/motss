is. If you think about this, could this include Any facility which 
has showers? like gyms and y[wm]ca's, sauna's, swimming pools. If

so you have a choice, let them have their bath houses and we keep
the parks, or let tham also have the parks. This is sort of like
the issue of abortions: there will always be people who will get
abortions, so do you let then do it in the back alleys with cloths
hangers, or do you let them in the hospitals?
