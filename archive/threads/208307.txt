A new nonfictional book on Gay male relationships has just been
published by Prentice-Hall.  The book; "The Male Couple - How
Relationships Develop"  covers all aspects of developing and
(more importantly) maintaining a stable and healthy relationship.

The book covers  couples from the first year (blending) thru
the period after 20 years (renewing).

This isn't a review, the book just arrived in town 3 hours ago!
A review will follow shortly after I establish a working relationship
for 20 years and have finished the book (which ever comes first!)
