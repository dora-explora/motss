New documentary coming
rrizzo@bbncca.ARPA (Ron Rizzo)
10/12/84
A movie documentary, "The Life & Times of Harvey Milk", is currently
playing in New York & is getting a wide & favorable reception.  Milk,
"the Mayor of Castro Street" (see Randy Shilts' biography of the same
name), was San Francisco's 1st openly gay Supervisor (the Board of
Supervisors actually govern the city), a popular democratic Bay Area
political figure, & camera shop proprietor.

Be sure to see it when it comes to your neighborhood theater!

					Ron Rizzo
