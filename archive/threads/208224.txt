Chicago Area Group Meets for Lunch
kinn@ihu1e.UUCP (Kevin Kinnear)
02/01/84
	A group of gay professionals in the West Suburban Chicago area has
scheduled an informal luncheon once a month.  The purpose of the luncheon is
to provide an opportunity for gay professionals in the area to get to know
each other in a professional setting.  We have been meeting for lunch for over
a year at various restaurants in the area.

	Most of the people in the group come from AT&T Bell Laboratories, but
there are representatives from other area companies as well.  The luncheon
group is not affiliated with any company at all.

	If you are interested in coming to one of the luncheons, send mail or
call me for more information.

Kevin Kinnear
