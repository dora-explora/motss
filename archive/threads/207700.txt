Golden State Gay Rodeo Assoc. Cocktail Party in San Francisco
rob@ptsfa.UUCP (Rob Bernardo)
02/14/85
If you live near San Francisco and have an interest in rodeo, horses,
western dancing/music, clogging, etc., you will definitely want to
mark your calendar for Friday, March 15, 7:30pm.

At that time, a cocktail party will be held in San Franciso
to bring together people in the SF Bay Area who are interested in starting
a Northern California chapter or Bay Area chapter of the Golden State Gay
Rodeo Association. The exact location will be announced in a later news item.

The purpose of this cocktail party is to get an idea of whether there is
sufficient interest in starting a local chapter of the GSGRA or whether
to just affiliate ourselves with the state-wide association.
-- 


Rob Bernardo, Pacific Bell, San Francisco, California
