Gay Rodeo in Los Angeles
rob@ptsfa.UUCP (Rob Bernardo)
02/14/85
The Los Angeles chapter of the Golden State Gay Rodeo Association
will be holding their first rodeo the weekend of March 29, 30, and 31.
The rodeo events will be held at the Los Angeles Equestrian Center,
Griffith Park, along with a country dance and music festival.

The GSGRA hotline can arrange your airline and accomodation reservations,
as well as offer information about the rodeo itself. The number is
(213) 438-8828, and is staffed 6pm - 9pm PST Monday - Friday.
Or write to GSGRA at P.O. Box 90873, Long Beach, CA 90804.

The GSGRA was formed about half year ago and is a member of the
International Gay Rodeo Association (with other member organizations
in Texas, Colorado, and Arizona).
