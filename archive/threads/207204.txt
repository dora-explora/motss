Last year several annotated lists of films shown during gay film
festivals were posted to the net.  (e.g. San Francisco and Boston
come to mind.)  I dutifully saved those lists for reference but 
lost them when one of our machines "bought the farm."  

Now a feminist group that I belong to is organizing a film festival
for Baltimore.  I think those lists and any others you might have
on hand could be useful to us.  (We are anti-racist, anti-militarist,
anti-imperialist, anti-sexist, and so forth; and we all have a 
wonderful sense of humor and a positive outlook on life, right?)

Please don't clog the net with postings that we have all seen before.
Better send me mail.  Thanks.
