This article is being posted for box9294:

Please move the discussion about the Bible and Christianity to net.religion.
Those discussions have little to do with being gay.  When I have my basic civil
rights, then I will worry about religion.  Until then, I am concerned with
finding an employer who does not discriminate on the basis of sexual
preference.  I am concerned with finding a landlord who does not discriminate
on the basis of sexual preference.  I am concerned with equal protection under
the law regardless of sexual preference.

After that, I will be concerned with being able to express
my affection with whom I choose in the privacy of my own home.

After that I might be concerned with arguing that God made me and that God
loves me and whether or not I am more of a sinner than someone else.
