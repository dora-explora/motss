Just to clear things up:

The Boston Gay Pride Parade is on SATURDAY, JUNE 15, not Sunday, June 16,
as had been previously posted.  Check the Bay Windows and GNC for all the
activities which will be happening throughout the week, starting on Friday
June 14.

There will be a lot of activity around the Mass. DSS (Dept of Social Service)
decision to NOT allow gay men and lesbians to care for foster children.  My
friends involved in organizing the parade ask that people bring signs protesting
this decision, and protesting the bad/negative treatment that we experiencing
at the hands of the "liberal" democratic party and people like MA governor
Dukakis. 

For those of you near Worcester, MA, there will be (I believe) Worcester's
first pride parade the week after the Boston parade.

"Come out, come out, where ever you are!!!"
