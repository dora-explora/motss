I, too, am starting a forwarding service for anonymous contributions to
the net.  Please begin your letters "PLEASE POST:" so that I can tell
that it is not personal mail for me.  If you do not include a pseudonym
I will assign you one that looks like a PO Box number.  I am starting
this service primarily for people of AT&T Bell Labs and AT&T Western
Electric, but I will forward articles for anyone.

My posting of an article does not imply that I agree with the article.
