But Sophie asked if there was a homosexual/heterosexual war with the
bisexuals caught in between.  Some might choose to characterize what is
happening as a war.  There are certainly many many gay and lesbian

a war? or simply part of the usual "domestic scene"?

adversary you might consider what goes on just simple oppression.
Perhaps by characterizing it as war you somehow give some credence
to the power of the oppressed group (although in a negative sense).
