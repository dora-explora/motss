The following lists of gay organizations came from the 1985 edition of the
"Encyclopedia of Associations" (published by Gale Research Co., Book Tower,
Detroit, MI 48226).  The full entries in the "Encyclopedia" contain much
more detailed information on the organizations listed.  You should be able
to find the "Encyclopedia" in any good reference library.

Unfortunately, Canadian groups are not included in the list.

If you write to any of these organizations for more information, be sure
and include an SASE.
